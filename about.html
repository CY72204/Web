About Me

Hello! Welcome to my corner of the internet! I am Chris Yeung, a recent graduate with a degree in Physical and Environmental Geography. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a passion for conducting geospatial data analysis, and I’m excited to transition into the professional world, where I can apply the knowledge and skills I’ve acquired.

My Strengths and Experience

During my time at university, I engaged in various projects and internships that honed my abilities in data analysis, map-making, and problem-solving. For instance, my team and I worked on creating a web map for locating restaurants of the users' selected cuisine in Toronto which has helped me develop a strong foundation in data analysis. In addition to my academic work, I am also proficient in tools, such as Python, HTML, Office, and G Suite, as well languages, such as English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. My hands-on experience in these areas ensures I can quickly add value to any team I join.
