Project 3: Restaurants and Food Markets Web Map

Developed a specialized web map focused on enhancing the culinary tourism experience in Toronto. This particular web map differentiates itself by offering a streamlined, user-friendly interface that emphasizes ease of use and informed decision-making through comprehensive filters. By meticulously curating restaurant, market, and food festival data, the map delivers concise, valuable insights, enabling tourists and city newcomers to explore Toronto's rich culinary scene with unprecedented clarity and efficiency.
